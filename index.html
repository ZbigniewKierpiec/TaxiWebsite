Saloon

Most popular and most economic, the saloon (Sedan) minicab is the versatile, all purpose cab with enough room for up to four passengers and moderate amount of luggage. If you feel that your luggage may not fit into a saloon taxi then you may wish to consider an Estate taxi (Station Wagon) which allows you more room for luggage or bulky items. For groups larger than 4 people, you need to hire an MPV (Minivan) taxi.

MPV

Our MPV vehicles are perfect for carrying baggage or business materials as they are able to stow up to eight suitcases and two items of hand luggage (standard cabin-sized dimensions). If you are interested in reserving one of our executive large taxis and have luggage, do get in touch and we will be happy to advise you on dimensions and capacity. The MiCabi Large Taxi range is a V-Class Mercedes or similar and will always be a stylish, practical option for airport transfers and local taxi journeys. The interiors are sleek with supple, comfortable upholstery and ergonomically designed seating. The interior consoles and technology are all to the highest standard to ensure we can meet your every need wherever possible.
